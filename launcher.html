WaTracker

Record

WaTracker hooks into the WhatsApp web app to repeatedly check if your friends are online. This information is collected and stored locally on your computer, so you can later visualize it and analyse your friends' activity.

Visualize

First of all, while recording you can see in real time who is using WhatsApp at the moment.

The information collected by WaTracker is of type: "Dan came online at 17:14" or "Rachel went offline at 03:00". This information can be displayed in beautiful "timeline" graphs. You can find out how much time people spend using WhatsApp. If they use it a lot you will also know when they go to sleep and when they wake up!
